X to off Axis
1 To Enable Culling
2 To Disable Culling
3 To Turn off Wireframe Mode
4 To Turn on Wireframe Mode

Light Controls:
Spacebar to enable/disable light

FPS Camera Controls:
Mouse to look around

W to move Forward
A to move Backward
S to move Left
D to move Right

Ship Controls:
Mouse to look around

W to Pitch Down
S to Pitch Up
A to Yaw Left
D to Yaw Right

Shift to increase thrust
Ctrl to decrease thrust

Press E to interact

Note: Fly into the sky to warp into space, Fly back into earth to warp back.
